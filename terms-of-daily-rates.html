  • Debt-to-income 43% or less

Important Daily Rate Disclosure

Sample rates are for informational purposes only and are not a commitment to lend. Rates, APR, fees, terms, and qualification may vary based on credit score, loan amount, property type, occupancy, down payment, lock period, and other factors.

Loans must be in “Ready for Underwriting” status by the Package Due Date in order to preserve lock terms. Package Due Dates are disclosed on your initial lock confirmation.

If a Package Due Date is missed, the loan will need to be relocked with worst case pricing.

Lock Period And Package Due Dates

  • 21 days — Package due in 6 calendar days from the date the loan is locked
  • 30 days — Package due in 10 calendar days from the date the loan is locked
  • 45 days — Package due in 15 calendar days from the date the loan is locked
  • 60 days — Package due in 30 calendar days from the date the loan is locked
